Reservations

Welcome to Temple Inn & Suites. Please read this before filling out the form. click here

  • We do not make reservations for Sundays.
  • Reservations are for people who go to the temple during their stay in our facilities. You must bring your current recommendation or special invitation.
  • We do not make reservations for minors traveling alone, there must be a person of legal age.
  • The reservations is subject to the selected Temple and Temple Inn & Suites availability.
  • Some of our services are free, others have charge. The total amount of the reservation must be paid before check-in.
  • When you submit your request it is not approved automatically. You will be contacted within 24 hours.
  • Check-in must be done after 2 pm.
  • Check-Out must be done before 11 am.
  • Reception after your sealing, call us or send this form.
  • Temple missionaries, call us or send this form.
  • Any question call us or send this form.
