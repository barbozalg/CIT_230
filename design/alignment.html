Alignment

Company Name

Scriptures of lds.org

How Used

As you see in the image, Alignment refers to the placement of all the design elements on a page. If you put the text at the top left corner of the page and then place your logo in the center of the page with images scattered around the edges, your readers won’t know where to look for specific information about your company.

But if you carefully arrange the elements on the page so that all the images and text align with the same invisible line, you’ll create a logical flow that pleases the eye.

As a general rule, the edges of your images should line up with the margins of your text. This principle generates clean lines and plenty of white space that appeal to your readers visually.
